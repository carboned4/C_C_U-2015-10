The C:I:A Building is the internationally acknowledged headquarters of the Agency, also receiving agents from other countries and bureaus.

It is made of 6 departments: Research and Investigation, Security, Field Operations, Business Management, Training Center and Administration.

Approximately 5,000 people work in the building, in shifts of 8 hours, to assure a total cover of every year’s 365 days and their 24 hours.

We at the Agency, as we call it, are working for your security. If you would like to work here or on field, don’t be afraid to join us!

The Agency and its roots
The C:I:A was created after WW2, in 1947, and its main purpose was to gather intelligence from missions. It couldn't enforce the Law, though.
But this was recently changed thanks to the 2011 Conference for Homeland Security in the state of San Andreas, due to the increase in terrorist attacks, weather crises and other situations we are not allowed to tell.
This new Central Intelligence Agency was led by Salvaz and Carboned4, two highly capable agents.
As they said in an interview: "We decided to create this clan, well.... mostly because no one had ever created one like this. We are commited to work for the security of the San Andreas state, in the MTA servers, so we started searching for people with a true C:I:A spirit. They had to be in charge but also willing to have fun in the cases. As a governmental organisation, we don't want people here just because. We want them to have a role in the community, to have an opininon... and fun."
